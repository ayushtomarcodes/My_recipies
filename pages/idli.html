Idli Recipe

Description

Idlis are steamed rice buns/cakes (?). It is a common South Indian breakfast/snack. Idlis are usually paired with sambar, chutney or any sauce based dish to your liking

Ingredients

  • sambar
  • Red Chutney
  • idli

Steps

  1. Soak rice for at least 6-7 hours
  2. Soak urad daal for 2 hours
  3. Soak fenugreek seeds for 1 hour
  4. Repeat and make as many batches as you require
  5. Serve hot with sambar and/or chutney!
